Game description:

The legendary game series are back to present the public with a completely new kind of adventure. For example, today you will have the opportunity to return to the world of the game Dyne Light 2 Remain Human, who fell under the onslaught of a virus and as a result of which most of the population turned into bloodthirsty zombies. Now the survivors must hide in small areas and defend themselves from misfortunes from the outside, along the way arranging a showdown among themselves. Sound crazy? But this is exactly what the game world looks like, and now it’s up to you to decide how exactly the environment will flourish, which gang will become dominant and whether people will find a cure for this dangerous disease.
